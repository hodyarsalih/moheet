About the Brand

DH__COUTURE operates across two strategic hubs: Dubai, where sourcing, partnerships, and trade are anchored, and Iraq, where the brand’s audience lives and shops. Products are procured through Dubai’s wholesale and luxury supply network and fulfilled via dedicated warehouse operations in Sulaymaniyah, Iraq, ensuring consistent availability for a nationwide customer base.

Reach & recognition

With millions of interactions each month across social platforms, DH__COUTURE is widely recognised among fashion-forward customers in Iraq. The brand’s digital footprint reflects sustained trust, repeat engagement, and a style language tuned to modern luxury.

Checkout

Moving Toward Cashless Shopping

Al Moheet Al Dahabi Goods Wholesaler L.L.C now supports secure online payments for a faster, safer, and more transparent purchase journey. Digital settlement reduces friction at handover, improves record-keeping for both retail and wholesale clients, and aligns with global best practices in e-commerce.

Customers who pay online can receive delivery within 24 hours through the Iraq distribution network—subject to order cut-off times and regional coverage—so you spend less time coordinating payment and more time enjoying your order.
